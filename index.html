Excellent balance of vowels and common consonants (S, L, T).

2
CRANE
Also highly rated by WordleBot

Contains 2 vowels and high-value consonants (C, R, N).

3

Variants: ALERT, ALTER, RATEL, TALER

Strong vowel presence with frequently used consonants.

4

Variants: RATES, TEARS

Covers 3 common vowels (A, E) plus high-frequency consonants S, T, R.

RAISE
Variants: ARISE

Contains 3 vowels (A, I, E) and 2 of the most common consonants (R, S).

To solve Wordle efficiently, follow these proven strategies:

  1. Start with a strong word: Use words like SLATE, CRANE, or STARE that contain common vowels and consonants.
  2. Maximize information: Your first 2-3 guesses should focus on gathering maximum information rather than solving immediately.
  3. Use elimination: Pay attention to gray (excluded) letters and avoid reusing them.
  4. Position matters: Green letters are in the correct position - keep them there in subsequent guesses.
  5. Relocate yellow letters: Yellow letters are correct but misplaced - try them in different positions.
  6. Use a Wordle solver: Tools like our Wordle helper can suggest possible words based on your clues, making you a better player over time by teaching patterns.
  7. Learn from WordleBot: The NYT WordleBot analyzes your games and suggests improvements.
  8. Practice regularly: Try to play Wordle more than once per day and improve your skills.
